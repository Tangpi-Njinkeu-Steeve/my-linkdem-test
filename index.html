The full story

What is Link Dem?

Imagine you lose your favourite bag, your phone, or a child gets lost in a crowd. People are often afraid to help — they don't know who to call. And you're afraid to write your number on a tag because strangers might misuse it.

Link Dem is your "secret shield": a QR code that connects people back to you — without ever exposing your personal number.

The contact information is visible for only 60 seconds after scanning. After that, it disappears automatically. The finder has to scan again to re-access it.

How do I use it?

  1. Enter the phone numbers of people you trust — your mum, dad, uncle, colleague.
  2. Click Generate to get your unique QR code image.
  3. Take it to a sérigraphie shop in Douala. They'll print it as a small durable sticker (typically 3,500–5,000 FCFA for a 1 cm cube with multiple stickers).
  4. Stick it on your phone back, laptop, school bag, official documents, or a bracelet for a child.

A kind person who finds your item scans the code, taps Call or WhatsApp, and your trusted contact arranges the return — your private number never exposed.
