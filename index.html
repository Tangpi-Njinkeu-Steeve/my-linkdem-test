What is Link Dem?

Imagine you lose your favorite bag, your phone, or even worse, a child gets lost in a big crowd. Usually, people are afraid to help because they don't know who to call, or the owner is afraid to put their phone number on a tag because bad people might see it.

Link Dem is like a "secret shield" for your phone number.

  1. It Hides Your Number: Instead of writing your phone number on your bag, you make a special "QR Code" (a black and white square picture).
  2. It Protects You: When someone scans that square with their phone, they don't see your number. They only see two big buttons: "Call" or "WhatsApp".
  3. It Disappears: The information only stays on their phone for 1 minute. After that, it vanishes! If they want to see it again, they have to scan the bag again.

How do I use it?

  1. You go to the website and type in the phone numbers of people you trust (like Mom, Dad, or an Uncle).
  2. The website creates your special QR Code square.
  3. You must download this picture and take it to a "Serigraph" (a printing shop). They will print it onto a strong sticker or a tag for you. Generally, it cost from 3500 - 5000 or more FCFA to have a 1 cm cube which will have on it multiple stick.
  4. Stick it on your phone, your laptop, your school bag, or even a bracelet for a child.

Now, if you lose your item, a kind person can scan the code and call your family immediately without ever knowing your private phone number!

This website in Cameroon is helping people to link back to their items since 2026.
